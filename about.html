Hong Kong, a metropolitan area and special administrative region of the people's Republic of China on the eastern Pearl River Delta of the South China Sea. History, Hong Kong became a colony of the British Empire after the Qing Empire ceded Hong Kong Island at the end of the First Opium War in 1842. The colony expanded to the Kowloon Peninsula in 1860 after the Second Opium War. Facts, #1 Hong Kong is famous for towering skycrapers, #2 Hong Kong means "fragrant harbour" and #3 Hong Kong's beloved Star Ferry started running in 1880. Attractions, #1 Hong Kong Harbor Night Cruise and Dinner at Victoria Peak,#2 Plove Cove Bicycling and Hiking Water Adventure, and #3 Afternoon Tour: Lantau Enlightenment Tour.
